# Privacy Policy — Personal Wellness Journal (WHOOP Integration)

**Last updated:** July 9, 2026

## Overview

This application ("the App") is a personal-use tool built to help a single individual (the developer) collect their own WHOOP data — including sleep, recovery, strain, and workout data — for personal journaling and self-reflection purposes related to health, exercise, and wellness.

The App is not distributed, marketed, or made available to the public. It is used solely by its developer, for their own personal data.

## What Data Is Collected

The App accesses the following data via the WHOOP API, limited to the developer's own WHOOP account:

- Sleep data
- Recovery scores
- Strain scores
- Workout and activity data
- Basic profile/body measurement data (e.g., height, weight, max heart rate), if requested

## How Data Is Used

Data retrieved from the WHOOP API is used exclusively to:

- Populate a personal journal for tracking healthy eating, exercise, and wellness habits
- Support personal reflection and pattern recognition over time (e.g., correlating sleep or recovery with habits and mood)

Data is not used for any commercial purpose, research, advertising, or any purpose beyond the developer's personal use.

## How Data Is Stored

Data is stored locally on the developer's own device(s) and/or in the developer's private, personal files. It is not stored on any third-party server, database, or cloud service accessible to others, except where the developer personally chooses to use their own private cloud storage for backup purposes.

## Data Sharing

Data collected through this integration is **not** shared, sold, licensed, sublicensed, or otherwise disclosed to any third party. No other individuals, companies, or services have access to this data.

## Data Retention and Deletion

The developer retains full control over their data and may delete it at any time. Access to the developer's WHOOP account via this App can be revoked at any time through WHOOP account settings, which will stop any further data collection.

## Security

Reasonable measures are taken to keep API credentials (Client ID, Client Secret, access and refresh tokens) confidential and secure, and these credentials are not shared with or exposed to any third party.

## Changes to This Policy

Since this App is for personal, non-commercial use only, this policy may be updated as needed to reflect changes in how the App functions. The "Last updated" date at the top of this document will reflect the most recent revision.

## Contact

This App is developed and used by a single individual for personal purposes. Questions about this policy can be directed to the developer directly.
